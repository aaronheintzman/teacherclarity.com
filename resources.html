The 90-Day Teacher Clarity Implementation Checklist

A free, practical roadmap for shifting your campus from task-completion to true Visible Learning — grounded entirely in John Hattie's research. With a 0.85 effect size, teacher clarity is one of the highest-leverage moves a school can make, and this checklist shows you exactly how to get there.

Organized into three phases — Foundations, Co-Constructing Success, and Ownership & Evidence — it guides your leadership team, instructional coaches, and PLCs through a sustainable 90-day rollout. Interactive checkboxes included so you can track progress online or print it for your team.
