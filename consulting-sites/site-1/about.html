Our Story & Mission

Teacher Clarity was founded after years of seeing the same problem: teachers working incredibly hard but without the clarity they needed to maximize their impact on students.

🎯

Our Mission

To make teacher clarity — the practice of being deliberately explicit about what students are learning, why it matters, and what success looks like — the standard in every classroom, not the exception.

🌟

Our Vision

A future where every teacher has the tools, coaching, and support to be crystal clear in their craft — and where that clarity becomes the foundation of equitable student achievement.

📖
Our Story

Built to Bridge the Gap

Teacher Clarity was founded after years of seeing the same problem: teachers working incredibly hard but without the clarity they needed to maximize their impact. The demands were real. The effort was real. But something essential was missing.

Our founder discovered John Hattie's research on Visible Learning and had a moment of recognition: "teacher clarity" — the practice of being explicit about learning intentions, success criteria, and feedback — was both one of the most powerful levers in education AND one of the least understood. Schools weren't failing for lack of effort. They were failing for lack of clarity.

Teacher Clarity was built to bridge that gap. We started with a single school, a single coaching relationship, and a single conviction: that when teachers are clear, students learn more — and that clarity can be taught, practiced, and sustained with the right support.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

These aren't words on a wall — they're the commitments that shape every client relationship, every workshop, and every coaching conversation we have.

🔍

Clarity

We believe clear learning intentions and success criteria are the foundation of every great lesson — and every great consulting engagement. We practice what we preach.

📊

Evidence

Everything we do is grounded in research — Hattie's Visible Learning, learning science, and the evidence base on effective coaching. We don't chase trends; we follow the data.

🤝

Partnership

We don't consult at schools — we partner with them. Every engagement is built on mutual respect, shared ownership, and a genuine belief in the educators we work with.

📈

Impact

Good intentions aren't enough. We measure our work against real outcomes — for teachers, for leaders, and most importantly, for the students in their classrooms.

🔭
Where We're Going

The Work Ahead

Teacher Clarity is growing — and we're committed to growing in ways that deepen our impact rather than dilute it. Here's what we're building toward:

Expanding our national coaching network to reach more schools and teachers
Developing an online Teacher Clarity certification program for coaches and teachers
Building a practitioner community for educators doing this work across the country
Deepening partnerships with districts committed to multi-year clarity work
Publishing research on the long-term outcomes of clarity-based coaching
