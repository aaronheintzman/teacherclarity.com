Why Teacher Clarity

The Research Is Clear. So Should Teaching Be.

John Hattie's meta-analysis of over 1,500 studies identified teacher clarity as one of the most powerful levers in education — yet it remains one of the least understood and most inconsistently practiced. That's the gap we were built to close.

Whether we're partnering for a single workshop or a multi-year coaching engagement, our goal is the same: to make clarity the standard in every classroom, not the exception.

Learning intentions and success criteria in every lesson
Observation, feedback, and modeling built into every cycle
Leadership coaching aligned to clarity practices
Progress tracking and outcome reporting throughout
