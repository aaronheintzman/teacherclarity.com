Our Services

Every engagement is tailored to your school's goals and context. We offer flexible partnerships — from a single workshop to a multi-year embedded coaching relationship.

What We Offer

Services for Every Stage of Growth

Whether you're just starting to build your PD culture or looking to deepen an existing system, we have the right support for where you are.

🎯

Professional Development

Research-backed, engaging PD workshops designed to shift instructional practice — not just check a compliance box. Topics include formative assessment, high-leverage teaching strategies, classroom management, and more.

  • Half-day, full-day, and multi-day formats
  • On-site or virtual delivery
  • Customized to your school's data
🤝

Instructional Coaching

Embedded, cycle-based coaching for individual teachers or grade-level teams. We observe, co-plan, model, and give actionable feedback — in real classrooms, in real time.

  • Individual teacher coaching cycles
  • Grade-level or department coaching
  • Coaching lab and model lessons
📐

Curriculum Design

Standards-aligned curriculum mapping, unit design, and lesson planning frameworks that give teachers clarity about what to teach, when to teach it, and how to assess it.

  • Scope & sequence development
  • Backwards design unit planning
  • Assessment design and alignment
📊

Data-Driven Instruction

Help your teams move from collecting data to using it. We build systems and habits for analyzing assessment data and translating it into differentiated instructional responses.

  • Data team facilitation and protocols
  • Assessment analysis frameworks
  • Action planning from data
🏫

Leadership Development

Support for principals, assistant principals, and instructional coaches on observation, feedback, and building a sustainable culture of instructional improvement.

  • Instructional leadership coaching
  • Observation & feedback frameworks
  • Distributed leadership systems
🗓️

Workshop Facilitation

Engaging, collaborative workshops on the topics your team needs most — from differentiation and equity to restorative practices and culturally responsive teaching.

  • Staff professional learning days
  • New teacher orientation programs
  • District-wide learning events
Our Process

How We Work With Schools

Every partnership follows a thoughtful process designed to ensure our work is grounded in your context from day one.

1

Discovery Call

We start by listening — to your goals, your challenges, and what success looks like for your school or district.

2

Needs Assessment

We review your data, observe classrooms (where possible), and talk with teachers and leaders to understand where support is most needed.

3

Custom Proposal

We design a tailored scope of work — timeline, format, and outcomes — built around your specific goals and budget.

4

Implementation & Iteration

We deliver, reflect, and adjust. Regular check-ins keep us aligned, and we share outcome data throughout the engagement.
